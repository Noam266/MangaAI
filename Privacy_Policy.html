🎵 Manga Music AI - One Piece Edition

Manga Music AI is a professional browser extension designed to create an immersive reading experience. Using a custom-trained AI engine, it analyzes manga panels in real-time to provide a dynamic, atmospheric soundtrack.

🚀 Overview

This extension features a locally integrated AI model specifically fine-tuned for the unique art style and emotional pacing of shonen manga. The AI "sees" the narrative flow and ensures the audio evolves perfectly with the story.

✨ Key Features

  • On-Device AI: Privacy-focused. No external APIs or cloud dependencies.
  • Dynamic Backgrounds: The UI reacts visually to the detected story mood.
  • Smart Persistence: Remembers your preferences and current state across sessions.
  • Manual Control: Includes 6 core emotional themes for full user customization.

🎭 Supported Moods

The AI is trained to trigger high-quality audio for: Calm, Sad, Tension, Action, Epic, and the rhythmic Drums of Liberation.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection: Manga Music AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (PII).
2. Local Processing: All AI analysis of manga panels happens locally on the user's machine. No images are uploaded to any server.
3. Permissions: We only request access to the active tab to analyze the visual layout of the manga being read.
