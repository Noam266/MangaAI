# 🎵 Manga Music AI - One Piece Edition

**Manga Music AI** is a smart browser extension that creates an immersive reading experience. It uses a custom-trained AI engine to analyze manga panels in real-time and play a dynamic soundtrack that perfectly matches the atmosphere of the story.

---

## 🚀 Overview
This extension is specifically optimized for **One Piece**. Unlike standard tools, it features a **locally integrated AI model** trained specifically to recognize the unique art style, panel layouts, and emotional beats of Eiichiro Oda's work. The AI "sees" what you read and ensures the music evolves with the narrative.

## ✨ Features
* **Built-in AI Engine:** No external APIs or cloud dependencies. The AI is trained to recognize manga panels and emotional moods directly.
* **One Piece Optimized:** Fine-tuned to detect specific shonen tropes and the iconic pacing of the One Piece manga.
* **Dynamic Backgrounds:** The extension popup UI reacts to the detected mood with glowing gradients and smooth transitions.
* **Manual Overrides:** Full control via Manual Mode to select any of the 6 core emotional themes.
* **Smart State Persistence:** The extension remembers your settings and the current mood even if you close the popup.

## 🎭 Supported Moods
The AI is trained to classify and trigger music for:
- 🌿 **Calm:** Exploration, world-building, and lighthearted dialogue.
- 🌊 **Sad:** Emotional backstories, tragic farewells, and "death" scenes.
- ⚡ **Tension:** Suspenseful plot reveals, stand-offs, and rising stakes.
- 🔥 **Action:** Intense combat, high-speed clashes, and power-up moments.
- 🏛️ **Epic:** Major transformations, grand reveals, and legendary "hype" scenes.
- 🥁 **Drums:** High-energy rhythmic sequences and the legendary **"Drums of Liberation" (Gear 5)** vibe.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Advanced Animations & Transitions), JavaScript (ES6+).
- **AI Logic:** Custom-trained image classification model designed to identify manga panel moods.
- **Architecture:** Manifest v3, Service Workers for audio management, and Content Scripts for real-time page analysis.

## 📦 Installation (Developer Mode)
1.  Download or clone this repository.
2.  Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3.  Enable **"Developer mode"** in the top right corner.
4.  Click **"Load unpacked"** and select the project folder.
5.  Open One Piece on your favorite manga site and start reading!
